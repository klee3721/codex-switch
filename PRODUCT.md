# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Developers who use Codex CLI with multiple ChatGPT-authenticated profiles on macOS. They need to see remaining usage quickly, switch accounts safely, and refresh state without interrupting terminal work.

## Product Purpose

Codex Switch manages local Codex auth profiles, shows 5-hour and weekly usage, and makes account switching available from the CLI, TUI, and native macOS status bar app. Success means users can tell account health and remaining quota at a glance, then act with minimal friction.

## Brand Personality

Precise, compact, utilitarian. The interface should feel native to macOS, quiet while idle, and immediately legible when usage limits matter.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative status items that waste menu bar width, centered content that leaves available space unused, and repeated app-logo marks where usage data should be the primary signal. Avoid generic SaaS card styling and visual noise that competes with account status.

## Design Principles

Glanceability first: the status bar must prioritize the one number users check most often.

Use the available surface: compact UI should still fill its reserved hit target instead of floating in the center.

Native over branded: preserve macOS conventions and readability before adding decorative identity.

Make state explicit: loading, stale, critical, and normal usage states need visible, non-ambiguous treatment.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain readable contrast for small status text, preserve accessibility labels for menu bar values, and avoid relying on color alone for critical usage state. Motion should stay native and minimal.
